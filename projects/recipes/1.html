My Red Velvet Cake Recipe.

Introduction

On this page you will find how to make a delicous red velvet cake. I chose this recipe because during all important tradional holidays like Christmas, New Years, Thanksgiving, etc, my mom bakes the most scrumptious red velvet cake for my family. we usually go to our cousins house for a big family gathering, but before that we make our red velvet cake and eat with just immediate family. My mother always says Whats food without family and I 100% agree with her, that is why I am sharing this recipe, to guide families to have there very own special cake.

Recipe, and Ingredients

Ingredients
Ingredient Amount
Red Velvet Cake Mix Full Bag
Water 1 Cup
Oil 1 Cup
Eggs 3

Total prep time is about 1 hour, serving is 1/10 of the cake.

Recipe
Steps Instructions
1 Heat oven to 350 degrees farenheit in a pan.
2 Mix Cake Mix, water, oil, and eggs in a large bowl.
3 Use a mixer on medium speed, or a beater by hand for 2 minutes and pour into pan.
4 Stick a toothpick into the pan, and bake until the toothpick inserted comes out clean.
5 Cool for 10 mintues before removing the cake from the pan.
6 (Optional) Cool completely before frosting the cake.

Tips and Tricks

  • Frost the cake to make it much more tastier!
  • You can make multiple cakes and stack them with frosting to make a delicious cake.
  • Bring all your ingredients to room temperature to make a much more spongy cake.
