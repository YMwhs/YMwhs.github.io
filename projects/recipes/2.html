Introduction

On this page you will find how to make some savory eggs and toast for you to enjoy. I chose this recipe because my grandmother would always make me eggs for dinner, instead of breakfast which sounds abnormal but to me it was just a part of my childhood. Now I plan to share this recipe with the world because it means the world to me and my grandma she always told me You have anything at any time, the only thing stopping yourself Which she told no lie in because I love having my eggs for dinner, and I hope that you do too!
