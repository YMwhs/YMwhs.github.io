Introduction

On this page you will find how to make some savory eggs and toast for you to enjoy. I chose this recipe because my grandmother would always make me eggs for dinner, instead of breakfast which sounds abnormal but to me it was just a part of my childhood. Now I plan to share this recipe with the world because it means the world to me and my grandma she always told me You have anything at any time, the only thing stopping you is yourself Which she told no lie in because I love having my eggs for dinner, and I hope that you do too!

Recipe, and Ingredients

Ingredients
Ingredient Amount
Eggs 3
Paprika 1 Tsp
Oil 1 Cup
Salt 1 Tsp
Pepper 1 Tsp
Wheat Bread 2 Slices
Recipe
Steps Instructions
1 Preheat pan to a medium heat with oil.
2 Crack 3 eggs into a medium bowl.
3 Add salt and pepper into the bowl with the eggs.
4 Whisk for a minute, or use a electric beater to mix the eggs.
5 Once done mixing, add the mix to the pan.
6 Take out a spatula or fork, and start to move around the egg as it forms to scramble.
7 Once the eggs are golden yellow, take them out of the pan and onto a plate to cool.
8 Sprinkle some paprika onto the eggs and let them cool.
9 Take out 2 slices of wheat bread, and toast them.
10 Now, take out the toasted bread, and enjoy with your eggs.

Tips and Tricks

  • Add more spice blends to make the eggs more flavorful.
  • Use a rubber spatula to stir more efficently and prevent sticking.
  • A non-stick pan will help with the clean up by a lot!
